# **App Name**: DentaSync

## Core Features:

- Smart Slot Optimizer: An AI-powered tool that analyzes clinician availability and patient patterns to suggest the most efficient turn allocations, minimizing clinical idle time.
- Interactive Odontogram: A digital visual representation of a patient's teeth for real-time tracking of procedures, diagnostics, and clinical history updates.
- Multi-Tenant Clinic Portal: Secure dashboard with differentiated access for Chief Doctors, Assistants, and Specialists to manage patient data and clinic schedules.
- Patient AI Concierge: A conversational tool that enables patients to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments via WhatsApp and Web without staff intervention.
- Push & WhatsApp Alerts: Automated notification system for real-time appointment confirmations, reminders, and patient follow-ups.
- Clinical Data Repository: A secure database for persisting sensitive patient documents, dental history, and digital prescriptions in the cloud.
- Productivity Analytics: Real-time generation of operational reports including attendance rates, recurring treatments, and specialist performance metrics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Clinical Navy (#2651A3), a deep and trustworthy blue evoking medical professionalism and stability.
- Background color: Porcelain Grey (#F5F8FA), a clean, high-brightness surface that emphasizes a hygienic, digital environment.
- Accent color: Aquatic Cyan (#39ACB8), an analogous hue that provides high contrast for call-to-actions and interactive clinical icons.
- Main font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif used for its neutral and machined look, ensuring clarity for dense clinical data and dashboard metrics.
- Precision line-art icons that maintain thin strokes to keep the interface looking lightweight and modern, centered around medical and scheduling motifs.
- A grid-based, 'dashboard-first' layout using ample whitespace to prevent information overload during fast-paced clinical operations.
- Minimal state transitions and micro-interactions for calendar movements to ensure a responsive feel without distracting the practitioner.
